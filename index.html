⚠ Before you start editing

This tool runs entirely in your browser — there is no server and no cloud sync. Your work is saved to localStorage on this device only, and can be lost if you clear browser data, switch browsers, or open the app on a different machine.

To save and restore your work, use the Save and Load buttons at the top of the header.

  • 💾 Save — downloads a single .json snapshot with every chart tab, activities, RACI assignments, definitions, attached documents, and the roster.
  • 📂 Load — opens a previously-saved .json file to restore your work (or to bring someone else's chart into your browser).
