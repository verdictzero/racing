Untitled RACI

Methodology

Read this as a matrix: 4 echelons × 6 process areas = up to 24 discrete RACIs.

Each cell defines who is R / A / C / I for that specific decision or handoff.

The Portfolio RACI sets the ceiling; lower echelons decompose accountability without relitigating it — which is why the tier above's structural A inherits down as I by default.

Legend

R Responsible — does the work
A Accountable — owns the outcome
C Consulted — two-way input
I Informed — one-way update

How to use

Each cell is one mini-RACI (a tier × a process area).

click a cell to open the people editor.

Every tier carries a structural A shown in the row header.

Inheritance: a tier's structural A auto-appears as I in the tier immediately below it (italic, prefixed ↑). Override per cell to C or exclude from the editor.

Autosaves to browser storage.
